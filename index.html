    REST WELL, REST ASSURED

    Be rest assured that in the event of travel restrictions resulting from the current COVID-19 situation, we will honour your booking, regardless of booking terms, with unlimited changes within 12 month from your check-in date (subject only to seasonal rate fluctuations).

    Welcome to the The Sandhya, an adults-only boutique beachfront hotel on the southern coast of Sri Lanka.

    Perched upon the white sands of Kabalana Beach, Ahangama, one of the best beach stretches and surf points on this island, our hotel offers 9 luxuriously-appointed bedrooms and suites providing guests with an understated yet quietly sophisticated atmosphere.

    The Sandhya is a design hotel and welcomes adults only.

    Casual Luxury In Paradise

    The awe-inspiring view of the ocean from every window and balcony may be one reason why you would never leave, but the delicious creations from our unique farm-to-table offering might be a reason you never return to your room.

    Lazily dangling your feet in the azure waters of the 15-meter plunge pool just off the living area, you will soon be hypnotised by the allure of the white tipped surf breaks just a few meters ahead. Grab a board and invigorated by some of Sri Lanka’s premier surf spots just outside of the hotel or just a few kilometers in either direction – world famous Rams Right at Midigama or the nursery waves of Weligama Beach are only minutes away.
